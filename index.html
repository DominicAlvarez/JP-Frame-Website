About

As an educator, JP Frame has dedicated 25 years
to the development of students at the
elementary, middle school, and high school
levels. His work with children began when
he started coaching at Chet Waggoner Little
League in South Bend, IN which led to his
pursuit of an Elementary Education Degree
from Indiana University.

He and his wife have 3 children and reside
in Kent, WA where he is currently employed
as a guidance counselor at one of the local
high schools.
